Temporary Weapon Enhancements

Using Spells, Magic Items, and Class Features

to add Enhancements to Manufactured Weapons

The ability to add temporary enhancements to weapons can help keep costs low and provides greater flexibility. That +3 shocking burst rapier is great, but when you are up against a troll, being able to add the flaming or corrosive enhancements is priceless. There are a large number of  spells, magic items and class abilities that can temporarily add enhancements to manufactured weapons, and they are listed below.

Keep in mind that no weapon can have an equivalent enhancement greater than +10, regardless of the sources of those enhancements. Also, identical enhancements do not stack unless they specifically state that they do.
