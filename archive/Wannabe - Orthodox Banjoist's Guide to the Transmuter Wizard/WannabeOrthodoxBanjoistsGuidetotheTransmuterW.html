Guide Concept:

This guide will focus on the NON-CLASSIC Transmuter: Polymorph, going into melee, playing on range and Polymorph your Familiar.

The Big Buffed Fighter - Polymorph his-self and go into melee kicking a#ses with his Natural Weapons.

The Glass Cannon - Polymorph his-self and stay on range, throwing his weapons on enemies.

The Familiar’s Buffer - Polymorph his familiar and stay behind it; both BBF and GC can do this.

RATINGS:

Rating will be the same as Treatmonk’s system, because is the more used and because i like to read his guides.

RED - A Very Poor Option. Should be avoided in nearly all cases.

ORANGE - A Mediocre, or uncommonly useful option. Generally not worth it.

GREEN - A solid option. Good in most instances.

BLUE - An excellent option. Should be taken in nearly all cases.

ROLES:

MELEE (THE BIG STUPID FIGHTER) - Your polymorph spells allow you to fit well this role; remember that your not the BSF, but the BBF (Big Buffed Fighter).

ABILITIES - STR>DEX>CON>INT>WIS>CHA

You want an excellent STR, a good DEX and CON, INT must be at least 11 for the 1ST-2ND level, WIS you’d better not Dump, CHA is your Dump stat.

You want Intelligence as follows:

lvl 1 and 2 = Int 11

lvl 3 and 4 = Int 12

lvl 5 and 6 = Int 13

lvl 7 and 8 = Int 14

lvl 9 and 10 = Int 15

lvl 11 and 12 = Int 16

lvl 13 and 14 = Int 17

lvl 15 and 16 = Int 18

lvl 17, 18, 19 and 20 = Int 19

RANGE (THE GLASS CANNON) - You are really made of glass, but your Polymorph spells will help you so much there.

ABILITIES - DEX>STR>INT>CON>WIS>CHA

You want excellent DEX, a good STR and INT, CON could be lower than the BBF assuming you’re attacking from afar, WIS is better to not Dump, CHA is your Dump stat as well.

Your INT can be like those of the BBF or more, your choice.

SPACE-WASTER (THE PARTY BALANCE/UNBALANCE) - This is the easier role to cover; are you looking at your dice, drooling?

MAGIC (THE MASTER OF PUPPETS) - You could easily become the party buffer and more.

ABILITIES - INT>DEX>CON>WIS>STR>CHA

You want excellent INT, a good DEX and CON, not Dumping WIS, STR and CHA become your Dump stats.

SPELLS & EQUIPMENT:

About spells and equipment there are many well-done guides that explain what to take for what you want to do.

I will only add this: avoid saveors#ck and blast spells, simply you don’t need ‘em.

Focus yourself on Shapechanging, Buffs, and Battlefield Control.

As a Big Buffed Fighter, assuming Polymorphing often, you don’t want Weapons nor Armors; focus on Enhancement that don’t disappear when you Polymorph, like Regeneration and similars.

As a Glass Cannon you should consider both Weapons and Armors.
