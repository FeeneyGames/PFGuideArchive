I have added the links and subject at the bottom - hope they don’t ruin your day...

Strength

  • Normally used for
  • Melee attacks rolls
  • Melee damage
  • Combat Maneuver Bonus (CMB)
  • Combat Maneuver Defense (CMD)
  • Skills
  • Climb
  • Swim

  • Base Class

  • Warpriest
  • Strength of Will (Warpriest 10, Strength blessing) Swift add str to saves against effects that would make you entangled, staggered or paralysed.

  • Prestige Class

  • Mammoth Rider
  • Wild Coercion (Prestige Mammoth Rider 1) Use strength in addition to charisma with wild empathy (works a intimidate)
  • Undaunted (Prestige Mammoth Rider 4) Add strength to DC for intimidating you in addition to wis
  • Combined Might (Prestige Mammoth Rider 8) 1 round add ½ steeds strength to dam in addition to own

  • Feats

  • Intimidating Prowess - add Strength to Intimidate checks in addition to Cha

  • Trait

  • The Flexing Arm (Faith Trait:Kurgess) use strength instead of dexterity to escape artist checks
  • Wrecking Wrath (Religion trait) 1/day add str to dam a second time

  • Items

  • Belt of Mighty Hurling, Lesser: use strength to hit with thrown weapons instead of dex
  • Belt of Mighty Hurling: use strength to hit with thrown weapons instead of dex
  • Composite Longbow/Shortbow - Add Strength to Damage

  • Normally used for
  • Ranged attack rolls
  • Armor Class
  • Reflex saves

  • Acrobatics
  • Disable Device
  • Escape Artist

  • Base Class

  • Cavalier
  • Champions Finesse (Cavalier ACF Daring Champion 1) Weapon finesse with light or one-handed piercing melee weapons.
  • Fighter
  • Deadshot (Fighter ACF Crossbowman 3) Dexterity to ranged dam (crossbow)  ½ dexterity to ranged attacks,
  • Greater Deadshot  (Fighter ACF Crossbowman 11) Dexterity to ranged dam (crossbow) when readied action
  • Trench Warfare (Fighter ACF Trench Fighter 3) Dex to dam for selected Firearm
  • Gunslinger
  • Bleeding Wound (gunsling 11, deed) use 1 grit do bleed dam = dexterity modifier

  • Pistol Training (Gunslinger ACF Pistolero 5) add dexterity to dam with one-handed firearms
  • Musket Training (Gunslinger ACF Musket Master 5) Add dexterity to dam with 2-handed firearm
  • Crossbow Training (Gunslinger ACF Bolt Ace 5) Dex to dam with Crossbow
  • Investigator
  • Talented Shot (Investigator ACF Steelhound 15) can select Bleeding Wound - see above. Dex to dam.
  • Rogue
  • Wand Adept (Rogue ACF Counterfeit mage 6)  Use Dex instead of Cha for UMD to activate Wands.
  • Rogue (Unchained)
  • Swashbuckler
  • Swashbucklers Finesse (Swashbuckler 1) us Dex instead of Str for to hit with light or one-handed piercing melee weapon
  • Dervish Finesse (Swashbuckler ACF Whirling Dervish 1) As Swashbucklers Finesse with scimitars.
  • Dervish Dance (Swashbuckler ACF Whirling Dervish 4) Add dex to dam when using Swashbucklers Finesse.
  • Inspired Finesse (Swashbuckler ACF Inspired blade 1) As Swashbucklers Finesse with rapier only.
  • Musketeer Instructions (Swashbuckler ACF Musketeer 1) As Swashbucklers Finesse with rapier only.
  • Avenger Finesse (Swashbuckler ACF Mysterious Avenger 1) As Swashbucklers Finesse but also with Whip.
  • Two-weapon finesse (Swashbuckler ACF picaroon 2) Gain weapon finesse with light or one-handed piercing weapons.
  • Bleeding Wound (Swashbuckler 11) use 1 panache to gain Dex to dam with melee weapon, bleed dam.
  • Bleeding wound (Swashbuckler ACF Flying Blade 11) se 1 panache to gain Dex to dam, bleed dam, using melee weapons, or thrown daggers and starknives.

  • Prestige Class

  • Aldori Swordlord
  • Deft Strike (Aldori Swordlord 2) Use dexterity to dam instead of strength w/ Aldori dueling swords, but not 1½ when 2HF, and not if using offhand

  • Feats

  • Agile Maneuvers (feat) Use Dexterity instead of Strength to CMB
  • Dervish Dance (combat feat) Use dexterity for to hit and damage with scimitar
  • Fencing Grace (not publiced yet) dex to dam?
  • Fury’s Fall (combat feat) use dexterity to trip attacks in addition to str
  • Indomitable Mount (local feat) Use dex based Ride skill instead of Mount’s save.
  • Slashing Grace (feat) use Dex to dam with a one-handed slashing weapon
  • Weapon Finesse (feat) Use dexterity instead of strength to attack rolls

  • Items

  • Agile (+1 weapon enhancement) use dexterity instead of strength to dam on finesse able weapon

Constitution

  • Normally used for
  • Hit Points
  • Fortitude saves

  • Base Class

  • Kineticist
  • Uses Constitution for many things(Future updates)
  • Witch
  • Constitution dependant (alternative class feature, Orc witch) use constitution for DC, highest spells known (not bonus spells), and anything int-based for Hexes Errated back to Intelligence

  • Prestige Class

  • Rage Prophet
  • Indomidale Caster (rage prophet 3, class feature) add constitution to concentration checks
  • Ragecaster (rage prophet 7) constitution to DC on spells while raging

  • Feats

  • Raging brutality (feat) use rage rounds to add constitution to dam
  • Raging Throw (feat) use rage round to add constitution to CMB (bull rush) and possibly to dam

  • Perfect Recall (Alchemist ACF Mind Chemist 2) Add intelligence x2 to Knowledge checks, and intelligence checks to remember

  • Throw anything (Alchemist 1) Add intelligence to dam with any splash weapons
  • Cleric
  • Tactical expertise (Cleric ACF Divine strategist 8) add intelligence to hit when flanking and on AoO, and 1/day (+1/day every 2 lvl) intelligence to a single d20 roll made as a readied action
  • Investigator
  • Ceaseless Observations (Investigator ACF Empiricist 2) Use Int instead of normal modifier for Disable Device, Perception, Sense Motive, Use Magic Device, and Diplomacy when gathering information.
  • Unfailing Logic  (Investigator ACF Empiricist 4) Use 1 inspiration point to use Int instead of wis to  saves vs. illusions that allow a save to disbelieve.
  • Magus
  • Arcane Accuracy (Magus Arcane, Magus class feature) Intelligence to hit as insight bonus 1 round uses arcane point
  • Flamboyant Arcane (Magus Arcana) Use the Int based arcane pool for panache.
  • Canny Defence (Magus, ACF Kensai 1) Intelligence to Ac while unarmored addition to dex, max 1 pr class level
  • Canny Defence (Magus, ACF Kapenia Dancer 1) as Duelist ability
  • Wand Mastery (Magus 3: Magus Arcana) Use intelligence for Wands DC instead of minimum to cast spell
  • Rod Wielder (Magus 3: Magus Arcana) Use intelligence to Caster level checks to overcome SR with rods
  • Rod Mastery (Magus 3:Magus Arcana) Use intelligence to Rod DC instead of minimum to cast spell
  • Arcane cloak (Magus 3: magus arcana) 1 arcane point to add Int to stealth and bluff to create a diversion to hide. For 1 min.
  • Spell Shield (Magus 3; Magus Arcana) Spend 1 arcane point to gain Int as a Shield bonus to AC until end of next turn, doesn’t stack with shield
  • Iaijutsu (Magus, ACF Kensai 7) Add intelligence to initiativ in addition to dex
  • Prescient Defence (Magus 9: Magus Arcana) spend 1 point from arcane pool, to gain Int to AC and reflex saves vs 1 target you just hit.
  • Critical perfection (Magus, ACF Kensai 9) add intelligence to critical confirmation rolls
  • Superior reflexes (Magus, ACF Kensai 11) add intelligence to the no. of AoO pr round stacks with combat reflexes
  • Iaijutsu focus (Magus, ACF Kensai 13) Add Intelligence to dam in surprise round against flat footed foes
  • Rogue
  • Kitsune Guile (Kitsune rogue1) add intelligence to bluff, diplomacy, disguise and sense motive
  • Chemical weapons (Rogue ACF Underground Chemist 2) Add Int to dam dealt by splash weapons, including splash damage.
  • Sorcerer
  • Bloodline Arcane (Sorcerer ACF, Sage ) use Intelligence instead of charisma to Sorcerer class features and effects
  • Swashbuckler
  • Inspired Panache (Swashbuckler ACF Inspired blade 1) Gain Int to panache pool in addition to Cha.
  • Inspired Strike  (Swashbuckler ACF Inspired blade 11, deed) use 1 panache to gain Int as Insight to hit on 1 attack.
  • Witch
  • White Hair (Witch ACF White Haired 1, 4) Use intelligence to dam with hair attack and Intelligence for CMB with Hair
  • Prehensile Hair (Witch Hex) hair limb that have a strength equal witches intelligence
  • Stone Spirit (Witch ACF Mountain Witch 1) Select Hex’s from Shamans Stone Spirits and use Int for their DC
  • Wizard
  • Hand of the apprentice (Wizard Universalist 1) use intelligence instead of dexterity for to hit with thrown weapons 3+int/day
  • Splintered Spear (Wizard wood specialist) make spear use intelligence instead of dexterity for to hit and intelligence to dam instead of strength 3+int/day
  • Power over Undead (Wizard necromancer 1) 3+intelligence turn attempt for command or turn undead only (DC 10+½ Wizard lvl +cha)
  • Spell Study (Wizard ACF 2, 6, 11, 16) 1/day Cast  a bard, druid and cleric spells as a Wizard spell using Int for DC and effect.
  • Spirit Hex (wizard ACf Spirit Whisperer 5, 10,  15) Use Int for chosen Spirit Hex
  • Heavens: (Guided star) add Int to all cha based skills
  • Lore: (Benefit of wisdom) Int to all Int based skills.
  • Nature: (Friends to animals): Animals within 30 feet gain Wizards Int to their saves
  • Spirit Link (wizard ACf Spirit Whisperer 1, 8, 20) gain a Shaman spirit, greater spirit and manifestation and use Int for it’s  abilities
  • Heavens 20: Manifestation gain Int to all saves.
  • Life 1: Spirit Channel energy 1+Int/day
  • Improved Scroll casting (Wizards ACF Scroll master 10) use Intelligence and own Cl for scrolls

  • Prestige Class

  • Cyphermage
  • Focused Scroll (Cyphermage, Cypher lore) 2x Intelligence to caster level checks with scrolls
  • Insightful Scroll (Cyphermage, cypher lore) use intelligence to DC from scrolls
  • Duelist
  • Canny Defence (Duelist 1) Intelligence to AC while unarmored addition to dex, max 1 pr class level
  • Razmiran Priest
  • Domain of Razmir (Razmiran Priest 1, 5) Use Intelligence to chosen domain powers (Charm, Evil, Law and Trickery only)
  • Shieldmarshal/Grandmarshal
  • Eye for detail (prestige: Shield/Grand Marshal 1) add intelligence to perception and sense motive in addition to wis
  • Sleepless Detective
  • Canny Sleuth (Prestige Sleepless detective 1) add intelligence to sense motive, perception and diplomacy to gather information in addition to normal stats
  • Student of War
  • Mind over metal (Student of war 2) intelligence to AC instead of dex

                

  • Feats

  • Focused shot (combat feat) Add intelligence to ranged dam (standard action only)
  • Kirin Strike (style feat) identify creatures with Kirin Style feat and use a swift action to add 2x Int to dam after having hit the creature with melee or ranged attack
  • Staff-like Wand (Wizard 11 discovery/feat) Use intelligence to DC of wands (and own CL)
  • Toppling Spell (metamagic) use Intelligence and CL for trip with force spells
  • Versatile Jinxer (feat) Use wisdom or intelligence instead of charisma to halfling jinx

  • Spells

  • Pilfering hand (magus 2, Wizard 2): use INT + CL for disarm or steal maneuvers instead of DEX/STR
  • Chain of Perdition (wizard 3): Use Cl+INT for dirty trick, drag, reposition and trip maneuvers, instead of bab+STR/DEX
  • Battering ram (Wizard 3) INT+Cl to Bull rush instead of STR+bab
  • Strangeling Hair (Wizard 3, Witch 3) grapple with INT+Cl instead of STR+bab
  • Bladed dash/bladed Dash greater (magus ⅖, bard ⅖ ) move 30 feet and make a single melee attack/attack all you pass and add int as circumstance to hit
  • Targeted Bomb Admixture (Alchemist 1) Bombs deal 2x Int dam instead of 1x Int Dam

  • Traits

  • Bruising Intellect replace Cha with Int for Intimidate
  • Student of Philosophy (Social) replace Cha with Int for diplomacy (persuade) and bluff (convince other a lie is true)  
  • Pragmatic Activator (magic) use replace Cha with Int for UMD.
  • Clever Wordplay (Social) use Int for one Cha based skill.

  • Item

  •  Astrolabe (1000 gp) Use int instead of wis to survival to avoid becoming lost

  • Smite Abomination (Cleric 5): Wisdom to hit single undead

  • Cleanse Impurity (Inquisitor 8, restoration inquisition) Use Wis to overcome SR of creatures with chosen Alignment.

  • Guarded Hearth (Cleric home subdomain) wisdom to saves and to hit, in warded area
  • Hand of the Acolyte (cleric magic domain) 3+wis/day wisdom instead of dexterity to ranged attack w/ melee weapon
  • Inspired Rhetoric (Inquisitor 1, reformation Inquisition)Use Wisdom modifier in place of your Charisma modifier when making Diplomacy, Intimidate, and Perform (oratory) checks.
  • Life Anchor (Inquisitor 1, Oblivion Inquisition ) add wisdom to stabilize checks within a 10 feet aura
  • Pack Tactics (Druid 8, Wolf Domain) When Flanking you may add Wis as untyped bonus to hit instead of +2 from flanking. 3+Wis/Day.
  • Recall (Cleric, memory subdomain) retry knowledge checks with wisdom as insight bonus, 3+wis/day
  • Rightful Infiltration (Inquisitor 1,Heresy inquisition) You use your Wisdom modifier instead of your Charisma modifier when making Bluff and Intimidate checks.
  • Surge (cleric Oceans subdomain) 3+wis/day make a Bullrush/drag with cleric lvl +wis
  • Tunnel Runner (cleric 8 cave subdomain) wisdom insight bonus to initiative, while undergound, 1 min/Cleric lvl(?)
  • Wind Blast (Cleric subdomain: wind) make blast and use cl+ wisdom to Bull rush instead of strength and bab
