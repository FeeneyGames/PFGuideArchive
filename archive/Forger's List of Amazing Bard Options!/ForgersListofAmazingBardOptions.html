The following list is intended to catalogue suggestions, items, feats, and traits that directly benefit the Bard.  Overlap with Skald is accidental.  Racial Options not yet added.

Please understand this is NOT a proper guide to bards.  This is a quick and dirty resource sheet for finding almost everything that specifically works AMAZING with bards or is just specifically designed for them.  Only a small amount of recommendations are included.

Summaries of feats/traits/items/spells are included to give you a quick idea of what will interest you.  Please read the actual descriptions (everything is hyperlinked) in order to maintain full understanding of options.

Combination of everything means at level 13+ the bard provides +8 on saves vs fear/charm (or better for himself with courageous) and a +9 on atk/dmg (better on himself with courageous), in addition to all other available benefits while using Inspire courage and Flagbearer together.  Bards are way more broken than people realize.

Consider a level of Swashbuckler.  Their weapon finesse ability won’t work with the elven branched spear, but panache and deeds offer a number of awesome benefits for a one level dip.

Inspired Swash (which focuses on the rapier) is an excellent option if you don’t want to devote yourself to being a pure buff based bard, as it provides weapon finesse and weapon focus with the Rapier at level 1, adds int as well as cha to panache pool total, and otherwise is still just swashbuckler.  Grants fantastic defensive options and a small pool of self skill buffs.

Another option is a three level dip in unchained rogue.  It offers both free weapon finesse and adding dexterity to damage in place of strength, both of which are things the Swashbuckler does not offer.  The rogue talent and sneak attack damage will also be useful.

Feats - the first two feats are optional improvements above and beyond what is offered by the basic buff bard options listed above.

Master Performer - Increase the bonus granted by bardic performance to allies by +1 (including effects like Inspire Heroics or Inspire Greatness)

Grand Master Performer - Further +1 increase to any bonus granted to allies by Bardic performance.  Yes, it stacks with Master Performer.

Lingering performance - Performance effects last 2 rounds after bard stops performing

Basic Harmony - better aid another checks if you both have this teamwork feat, -5 penalty for failure.

Compelling Harmonies - expend your own bardic performance to boost save dc of ally with this feat’s performance by +2.

General, Teamwork

Basic Harmony, Compelling Harmonies,Perform (keyboard, percussion, sing, string, or wind) 10 ranks, bardic performance or raging song

When an ally with this feat is within 60 feet and uses a bardic performance or raging song that grants allies a morale bonusor competence bonus, you may perform the same bardic performance or raging song to increase those bonuses by +1. This increase only lasts as long as you and your ally each maintain the same performance, and cannot be extended with effects like Lingering Performance. Additional bards with this feat beyond the second don't increase the bonuses further.

Items

Robes of the Resplendent Thespian - disguise armor as normal clothing, Bards get SR 18, +4 resist to saves, +2 CL to overcome spell resistance

Perfect Golden Lute - +10 perform string checks with this instrument, non bard can use countersong, fascinate, or distraction as 1st level bard for rounds = charisma mod.  Bard gets +2 DC all bardic performances, +6 rounds of perform a day.

Lyre of Building - once/day negate all attacks (including spell effects like disintegrate) against inanimate construction for 30 minutes.  Once/week can grant super construction.  Causes 30 minutes of work by one person to equal 3 days of work by 100 people.  Must make dc 18 perform (string) check every hour to continue providing benefit, once performance is stopped or check fails, must wait a week to use the lyre again for this purpose.

Bracers of the Glib Entertainer +5 perform checks, glibness 1/day

Helm of Telepathy - detect thoughts at will, send messages, send suggestion 1/day

Poet’s Cloak - Raging song as 4th level skald!

Dervish Sikke - Improve Bardic knowledge by +5 level, improve Inspire Courage/Competence by +1 (not level dependent)

Doomharp - use Dirge of Doom at lower level or at correct level increase range to 60ft

Book of the Loremaster - 3/day +5 to single knowledge check when taking 10 or 20, bards only

Rallying - Increase morale bonus from ability that grants saves vs fear to +6

Ring of Wizardry - double the class granted spells per day of a given spell level (1-4)

Tuned Bowstring - Maintain bardic performance while firing bow, use bow as masterwork instrument, add bow’s enhancement bonus to perform checks

Scarf of the Suggestive Dance - +5 perform dance, +1 dc fascinate and suggestion bardic performances

Strings of Spell Storing - similar to Ring of Spell Storing, can store up to 5 levels of spells.  DC 15 perform string check allows anyone playing the instrument the strings are equipped to to cast spells out of them.  These are a 3.5 item, use with caution, check with your DM beforehand.

Circlet of Persuasion - +3 to all charisma based skills

Lesser Metamagic Rod of Extend Spell - Good for many common Bardic buff spells, doubling their duration to last through the whole fight and then some.

Spells

Shadow Bard - Create an illusion of a bard who performs a bardic performance using your stats.  Does not use your rounds of bardic performance.

Virtuoso Performance - at cost of 3/1 rounds of bardic performance, maintain two bardic performances at once for duration of spell.
