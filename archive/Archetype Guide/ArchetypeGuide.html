Structure of Ratings

Name of the archetype & link to appropriate d20PFSRD /  ArchivesofNethys page

Ratings for Dip Power and Versatility

Ratings for Full Power and Versatility

Great (+2), Good (+1), Average (0), Bad (-1) and Terrible (-2)

Not all guides have rankings for all categories.

Reasoning and evidence supporting the rating.

Power: Rating based on the archetype’s overall strength in comparison to the base class.

Versatility: Rating based on the archetype’s ability to adapt to different situations, and its overall ability to do more than one action well.

Dip: Dip indicates that an archetype is being rated on its power when multiclassing.

[Dip]: [Dip] indicates that an archetype has uses for multiclassing, but no general rating.
