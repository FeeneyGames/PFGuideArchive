This is a list of the spells you can cast via your summoned monsters. This list only contains those that would affect something outside your summoned monster. For instance, invisibility (self only) is not on the list, neither is mirror image. Some spells require some work to use, the constant detect SLAs for example, requires you to communicate with your summoned creature.

This is mostly a copy/paste job from PFSRD, but this means that there are links to most of the spells. Just click on the spell name and then click on the link.

The list only includes spells from creatures from the summon monster list at PFSRD, excluding alternatives from adventure paths.
